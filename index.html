AgroLoaf Ghana Limited

Agroloaf Ghana is the trusted name behind the NOVA TERRA brand, serving as the sole importer of this premium hard wheat flour for many years. Our commitment has always been simple — deliver a product bakeries can rely on and provide the consistency that customers love.

Over the years, we’ve built strong relationships with commercial and artisanal bakeries across the country by supplying flour that performs every day, every batch. NOVA TERRA’s unmatched quality has helped bakeries achieve better dough strength, improved loaf volume, and consistent results that keep customers coming back.

At Agroloaf Ghana, satisfaction is not just a promise — it’s the foundation of everything we do. From reliable supply to product education and technical support, we ensure our partners get more than flour; they get a dependable partner dedicated to their growth.

Advanced Production

Our state-of-the-art production facilities utilize cutting-edge technology to ensure the highest quality standards. We employ automated systems and rigorous quality control processes to deliver excellence in every product.

With a focus on efficiency and sustainability, our production line is designed to minimize waste while maximizing output. Our experienced team oversees every step of the manufacturing process.

Smart Storage Solutions

Our modern storage facilities are equipped with climate control and advanced inventory management systems. We ensure your products are stored in optimal conditions with 24/7 monitoring and security.

From small-scale storage to large warehouse solutions, we provide flexible options tailored to your business needs. Our logistics team ensures seamless integration with your supply chain.
